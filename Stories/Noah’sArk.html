Noah’s Ark

God saw that the world had become full of sin, but Noah loved and obeyed Him. God told Noah to build a huge ark because a flood was coming.

Noah trusted God and built the ark just as He said. He brought his family and two of every animal onto the ark.

Rain fell for 40 days and 40 nights, covering the whole earth. But God kept Noah, his family, and the animals safe inside the ark.

After the flood, God placed a beautiful rainbow in the sky as a promise that He would never flood the whole earth again.

Genesis 9:13 – "I have set my rainbow in the clouds..."
