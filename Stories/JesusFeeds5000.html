Jesus Feeds the 5,000

One day, a huge crowd of people followed Jesus to hear Him teach. There were over 5,000 people, and they stayed all day listening to Him.

As it got late, the disciples were worried. “These people are hungry,” they said. “We don’t have enough food!”

A young boy had five loaves of bread and two fish. Jesus took the food, thanked God, and began to share it.

Something amazing happened! The food kept multiplying. Everyone ate until they were full—and there were even leftovers!

Jesus showed that God can provide more than we could ever imagine.

Lesson: Trust Jesus—He can provide for all your needs!
